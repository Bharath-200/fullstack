<i>In 2018, he made his Telugu debut with <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agnyaathavaasi">Agnyaathavaasi</a>, which has 6 tracks, a fast-paced massy introductory track Dhaga Dhagamaney sung by Anirudh himself, a sweet melody Baitikochi Chuste song ruled by the guitar chords and enchanting flute, It was an instant hit among youngsters with its quirky lyrics and a catchy foot-tapping tune, Gaali Vaaluga sung by Anirudh was also instant hit among music lovers. Niranjana Raman has sung a beautiful classical number, Swagatham Krishna, the inclusion of the guitars and synths gives an enchanting experience throughout the track. Nakash Aziz has crooned peppy number AB Yevaro Nee Baby and<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pawan_Kalyan"> Pawan Kalyan</a> has sung a folk number Kodakaa Koteswar Rao. The whole album has been received well.[72][73] His BGM received praises for his fresh sound.[74][75] Next, he composed for <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suriya">Suriya's</a> Thaanaa Serndha Koottam, which was a remake of Special 26. The song "Sodakku" was an instant hit among the masses. After that, he composed for Nayanthara's Kolamavu Kokila, which was well received and was praised for being unique. The song "Gun Kadhal" became popular among Anirudh fans. The first single of Petta titled "Marana Mass" was an instant hit.[76] On 10 December, the full album for Rajinikanth's Petta was released by Anirudh. His next project was Nani's Telugu film Jersey, which is directed by Gowtam Tinnanuri. He later appeared in Gang Leader which was directed by Vikram K Kumar. This marked his second collaboration with Nani. After that he appeared in Darbar, which was directed by AR Murugadoss. The song "Chumma Kizhi" sung by legendary singer SPB was a hit, and rapper Yogi B was roped in for "Thani Vazhi". He then composed one of the songs of the soundtrack for Dharala Prabhu, the title track, which garnered success. In 2020, he collaborated with Vijay for the 2nd time in Master, directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. The first single, "Kutti Story", sung by Vijay himself, received great praise and became popular around the world for its Tanglish lyrics. The second single sung by Gana Balachander, "Vaathi Coming", became an instant viral hit universally, with the kuthu song collecting 330 million views on YouTube, breaking many records rapidly from the get-go. This is the most viewed song composed by Anirudh as of yet. He also came to work on the soundtrack of Doctor, directed by Nelson Dilipkumar. The first single "Chellamma" was a hit among the youth with the fun and romantic lyrics, collecting over 100 million views on YouTube.[77] In 2021, he worked with Vijay[78] for the 3rd time in Beast directed by Nelson, whom he also worked with for the 3rd consecutive time.

In 2022, Anirudh scored hits with films like, Beast and Vikram, with the latter marking his first release with Kamal Haasan, although the duo collaborated for Indian 2 first which is yet to be released.[79] The first single of Vikram titled "Pathala Pathala" received several million views from Kamal Haasan’s fans and was the most viewed song on YouTube.[80] The song also received praise for Kamal Haasan’s dance performances which went viral. Despite facing controversies from the song, it became an instant hit.[81] He also scored hits from films like Don and Kaathuvaakula Rendu Kaadhal. The songs from the Don received several million views and was an instant hit.[82] The first single of Kaathuvaakula Rendu Kaadhal titled "Two Two Two" was also a hit on YouTube.[83] He is also composing music for Dhanush starrer Thiruchitrambalam which marks his fifth collaboration with the actor after 3, Velaiyilla Pattathari, Maari and Thanga Magan.[84] The first single of Thiruchitrambalam titled "Thaai Kelavi" was released on 24 June 2022 and received several million views which also became very viral and also became a huge hit.[85] Although the song faced some controversies.[86] A social activist has lodged a complaint against the makers of the film asking them to change the lyrics of the song,[87] as the lyrics of the song are shown to be disrespectful to the elders.[88] The second single titled "Megham Karukkatha", was released on July 15, 2022 which also received positive reviews.[89] The third and the fourth singles of Thiruchitrambalam titled "Life of Pazham" and "Thenmozhi" which was sung by Anirudh himself for the former song and Santhosh Narayanan for the latter were released on 27 July 2022 and 30 July 2022 respectively.[90][91] The entire soundtrack of Thiruchitrambalam was released on 31 July 2022.[92] While Academy Award-winner A. R. Rahman was reportedly approached by Atlee to compose for his Bollywood film with Shah Rukh Khan,[93] Rahman subsequently turned down the offer and Anirudh was chosen to compose for the film titled Jawan. An announcement teaser featuring Anirudh's score was released in June 2022.[94] His previous Hindi films were David and Jersey, the former featuring just a single song by him Yun Hi Re and the latter featuring film score by him, mostly reused from his score for the original Telugu film on which the 2022 film Jersey was based upon. Thus Jawan marks the first full-fledged debut for Anirudh in Bollywood as a song and score composer. He is also set to compose music for Rajinikanth’s 169 th film titled Jailor which will be directed by Nelson. The film will also mark Anirudh’s third collaboration with Rajini after Petta and Darbar and also with Nelson after Kolamaavu Kokila, Doctor and Beast.[95] He is also set to collaborate with Ajith for his 62nd film tentatively titled AK62 directed by Vignesh Shivan which marks his third collaboration with Ajith after Vedalam and Vivegam.[96] He is also set to compose music for Jr.NTR’s untitled 30 th Telugu film tentatively titled NTR30 which is directed by Koratala Siva.[97]
